// The Artists

Built with borrowed magic

This world isn't ours alone. We found most of these artists through itch.io, and their work shaped it as much as ours did.

Chierit: Gatsby's wolf form! Plus the Tank, Ranger, and a bunch of other enemies
Alejdark: The stunning cover art from Red Potion Studios
Otsoga: Zarah, and a lot of what tries to kill her Untied Games: The VFX that makes everything hit harder KBPixelArt: Most of the world you'll meet along the way Szadiart: The backgrounds you get lost in

And so many more. Thank you for helping make this world feel alive.
